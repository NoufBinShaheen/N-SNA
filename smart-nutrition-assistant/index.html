Frequently asked questions

Is this medical advice?

No. Smart Nutrition Assistant provides educational information and generalized guidance. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider for personal medical decisions.

Does it support chronic conditions?

Yes. The assistant can consider dietary patterns for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, kidney disease, PCOS, IBS, and more. Always validate plans with your clinician.

How does the demo work?

The demo runs fully in your browser with a rules-based assistant. The production app connects to an AI API for higher-quality responses.
